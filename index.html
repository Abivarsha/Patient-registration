Our Specialties

Cardiology

Abi Hospital’s Cardiology Department offers world-class care for heart conditions such as coronary artery disease, arrhythmias, and heart failure. We provide advanced diagnostics like ECG, echocardiogram, and angiography, along with expert treatment plans ensuring faster recovery and long-term cardiac wellness.

Neurology

Our Neurology Department specializes in diagnosing and managing disorders of the brain and nervous system, including stroke, epilepsy, and Parkinson’s disease. Equipped with advanced MRI and EEG facilities, we ensure precise care and rehabilitation for complete neurological recovery.

Orthopedics

Abi Hospital’s Orthopedic Department treats bone, joint, and muscle disorders with advanced surgical and non-surgical techniques. From fractures to joint replacements, our specialists ensure swift recovery through expert care and physiotherapy support.

Pediatrics

Our Pediatrics Department provides compassionate care for children, focusing on growth monitoring, vaccinations, and illness prevention. We create a comforting environment for every child while ensuring their overall health and well-being.
